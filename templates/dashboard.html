Over 50,000 children are reported missing in Nigeria each year. While many are found quickly, some become long-term missing cases. You can make a difference. The Child Report System helps law enforcement locate missing children faster.

Here's how you can help:

  1. Report missing children: If you suspect a child is missing, report it immediately to the nearest police station or through the Child Report System.
  2. Spread awareness: Share this message with your friends and family to raise awareness about the issue of missing children.
  3. Support organizations: Donate to or volunteer with organizations that work to find missing children in Nigeria.

Note: Details listed for each missing child are based on what was known at the time of their disappearance. Height, weight, and hair colour may have changed.
