Who Are We?

Child Alert Hub is a web-based platform dedicated to raising awareness about missing children and facilitating their safe recovery. The platform collaborates with trusted authorities, such as the police, to provide verified and up-to-date reports on missing child cases. Users must log in to access detailed information, ensuring the security and credibility of the system.
Key features of the Hub include

Alerts and Reports

Displaying critical details about missing children,
including names, images, and last known locations.

Community Engagement

Allowing users to share information, report
suspicious activities.

User Authentication

A secure login system for public users and
authorized personnel.

Real-Time Updates

Immediate notifications for new or resolved cases.

The primary goal of Child Alert Hub is to create a centralized and reliable source of information, fostering community involvement and aiding in the safe return of missing children.

Mission Statement

To provide a reliable, secure, and accessible platform that bridges the gap between the public and trusted authorities, ensuring timely dissemination of missing child alerts to foster community vigilance and aid in swift recovery efforts.

Vision Statement

To create a globally trusted hub that empowers communities through collaboration and technology, striving toward a future where no missing child is left unaccounted for and safety is a shared responsibility.
